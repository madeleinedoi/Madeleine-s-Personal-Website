About Me

Madeleine fell in love with Computer Science in her freshman year of high school. She is continuing her passion into a TEALS-taught post-AP course and will further pursue her journey of CS into college. Although she has coded primarily in Java, she is also familiar with other programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. She completed a 8 week long internship at CDK Global for the last 2 years. During her internship she was trained on and utilized JavaScript, CSS, HTML, GitHub, and Heroku to complete an application for the company. She is currently a co-leader of a student-founded and run non-profit organization, Women Advancing Tomorrow's Technologists (WATT) that teaches middle school girls to code. Having attended an all-girls high school, women empowerment and interest in STEM is very important to her. She has passion for social justice and being an Mukilteo Youth Council advocate for mental health. She is leading a mental health project named “Mukilteo Stands Behind Youth” to ignite conversation among her community regarding mental health. She would love to combine her passions of social justice and computer science to pursue an inspiring job in the ever changing environment of technology.seasonal ingredients.
